We do not sell personal information or share it with advertisers.

Sharing & Third Parties

We may share limited data with infrastructure or analytics providers who help deliver the Services, subject to confidentiality agreements. Data may be disclosed when required by law or to protect users and the platform.

Your Choices

  • Update profile information via the account settings dialog.
  • Request account deletion or data export by emailing support@notsolong.com.
  • Adjust browser or device settings to control cookies and local storage.

Data Retention & Security

We retain information only while it is necessary for the Services or to satisfy legal requirements. Access is restricted to the minimal operations team, and we implement industry standard protections such as encryption in transit and least-privilege credentials.

Changes

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date shows the most recent change. Material updates will be highlighted within the product when possible.
